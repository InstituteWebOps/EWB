Unleashing Great Minds on Great Problems

Engineers Without Borders IIT Madras builds a better world through engineering projects that empower communities to meet their basic human needs and equip leaders to solve the world’s most pressing challenges. Our 16,800 members work with communities to find appropriate solutions for water supply, sanitation, energy, agriculture, civil works and structures.

EWB-IIT Madras, India members have impacted more than 2.5 million lives.
